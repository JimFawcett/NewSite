"We want to make our programs elegant and simple, make their intents clear, and make them quick and reliable."
- "First Things" blog.
The Software Development (SWDev) track is concerned with the process, tools, and artifacts of developing software.
  • Process is a script for producing documents and tasks used to support developing software.
  • Tools make the tasks more efficient and reliable.
  • Documents describe both the process and artifacts of the development.
The most important themes are:
  • Software Design
    Specification, structure, public interfaces
  • Implementation and Test
  • Deployment
