Test Introduction

These test pages are shown without much explanation. They've explored ideas for this site to see what works. Some have been rejected and some have led to implementations that are an integral part of the site. To explore design and implementation of a test, open the test page, right-click, and select: This Frame > View Frame Source Any of the included stylesheets and scripts can be opened by clicking on links for those in the Source. Some of the test ideas that appeared to be useful for site operations have been developed into components and explained in much more detail. You will find them in the "Components" list available from the left panel of this view.
